# Ethical Button

**Purpose:** expose meaningful human consequence and add the least friction required for informed agency.

## States

| State | Example | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Notice | Publish · Public audience | Impact is visible |
| Resist | Publish to 384 people | Consequence is explicit |
| Hold | Hold for one breath | Deliberate commitment |
| Confirmed | Published · Undo | Result and recovery |

## API

`size: M | L` · `state: Notice | Resist | Hold | Confirmed` · `label: string` · `metadata?: string` · `showConsequenceMeter?: boolean`

## Proportionality rule

Inform before resisting. Add a hold only for high-impact commitment. Always provide a path to cancel, revise, or leave without punishment.

## Do not

Use this family to increase conversion, retention, subscriptions, or consent through fatigue. Ethical friction protects the user; it does not protect the funnel from the user.

## Accessibility

State the consequence in plain language before resistance begins. Provide a non-hold alternative for motor and switch access.
